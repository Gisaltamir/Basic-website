What we do?

At Pawsitive Embrace, we offer a unique and heartwarming service that not only fills your heart with joy but also makes a meaningful impact on the lives of animals and our community. Our primary service is providing one-hour sessions of animal hugging in a cozy and comfortable setting.

Here's What Set Us Apart:

  • Animal Hugging: Spend quality time hugging and bonding with our loving animals. It's an experience that brings immeasurable comfort, joy, and emotional wellness to your life.
  • Supporting Our Mission: The money you spend on our animal hugging sessions goes towards funding our refugee center. We are committed to providing a safe and nurturing environment for animals in need. Your support directly contributes to this cause, making a difference in the lives of both animals and our community.
  • Adoption Opportunities: If you form a special connection with one of our animals during your session, you have the opportunity to adopt them. By doing so, you not only bring a furry friend into your life but also provide a loving forever home for an animal who truly needs it.
